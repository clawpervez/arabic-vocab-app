Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how العربية بين يديك (Arabic Vocabulary Flashcards) ("the App") handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App operates entirely on your device. No accounts, no sign-ins, no tracking.

Data Storage

All data (your progress, streak, and card history) is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

Internet Access

The App may load web content via a browser (Trusted Web Activity). The content is hosted at clawpervez.github.io. No personal data is sent to this server.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
